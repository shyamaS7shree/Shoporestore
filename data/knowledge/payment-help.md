# Payment Help

Shopore payments may include online payment methods such as cards, UPI, and supported payment gateway options.

If a payment fails, customers should check their card, UPI, internet connection, and bank app, then try again.

If money is deducted but the order is not confirmed, customers should save the payment reference and contact support. The payment provider or bank may take time to update the final status.

For paid orders that are cancelled before shipping, refund processing starts after cancellation. Refunds usually take 5-7 business days, depending on the bank or payment provider.

For returned paid orders, refunds usually take 5-7 business days after the returned product is received and checked.

For Cash on Delivery orders, no online refund is needed before delivery because no online payment was collected. If a customer paid cash after delivery and later returns an eligible item, they should contact support through the Contact page for refund help.

If a customer needs urgent refund help, they should contact support with their order number, registered phone number, email, and payment reference if available.

Customers should never share OTP, card PIN, CVV, or full payment credentials with anyone.
