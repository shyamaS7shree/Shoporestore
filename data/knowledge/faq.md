# FAQ

## What is Shopore?
Shopore is an online shopping site for fashion, beauty, home, kids, and GenZ products.

## Do customers need an account?
Customers can browse products without an account. To manage profile details, wishlist, orders, and saved information, customers should login or signup from the Profile button.

## Where can customers find products?
Customers can use the navbar search for brands and products. They can also browse Men, Women, Kids, Home, Beauty, and GenZ categories.

## How can customers contact support?
Customers can use the Contact page and include their name, email, phone number, order number if available, and a clear description of the issue.
