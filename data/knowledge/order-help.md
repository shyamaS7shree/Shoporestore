# Order Help

Customers can track order status after login from Profile > My Orders. Open the order to see whether it is placed, paid, processing, shipped, delivered, cancelled, refund processing, or refunded.

The chatbot cannot see a customer's private live order status. If a customer asks for the exact status of their order, tell them to check Profile > My Orders or contact support with their order number, registered phone number, and email.

Customers can cancel an order from Profile > My Orders before the order is shipped. Open the order, choose cancel, select a reason if asked, and confirm cancellation.

No OTP is required to cancel an order in Shopore. Customers should be logged in to the account that placed the order.

Orders cannot usually be cancelled after shipment. If the order is already shipped or delivered, customers should wait for delivery and then use the return option if the product is eligible.

For paid orders, cancellation starts refund processing. Refunds usually take 5-7 business days after cancellation or after the returned product is received and checked.

For Cash on Delivery orders, there is no online refund before delivery because no online payment was collected. If a COD order is cancelled before delivery, the order is simply cancelled. If the customer paid cash after delivery and later returns an eligible product, they should contact support through the Contact page for refund help.

If a customer needs money back urgently, they should contact support from the Contact page with the order number and payment details. Support can check the case, but bank or payment provider timelines may still apply.

If a size does not fit, feels too tight, loose, or the customer received the wrong size, they can request a return from Profile > My Orders if the product is eligible. The item should be unused, unworn, undamaged, and kept with original tags and packaging.

For exchange requests, customers should contact support through the Contact page if exchange is not shown in My Orders. They should include order number, product name, size received, and size needed.

If a product is damaged, wrong, missing, or defective, customers should contact support as soon as possible with order number, photos or video if available, and a clear description of the issue.

Customers should not share payment OTP, card PIN, CVV, or banking passwords with anyone. Shopore support does not ask for these details.
