# Shipping Policy

Customers can check delivery availability by entering their PIN code on product or cart pages where PIN code checking is available.

Delivery timelines can vary by location, product availability, courier partner, and payment confirmation.

Order status and delivery updates are available from Profile > My Orders after login.

If delivery is delayed, customers should check My Orders first and then contact support with their order number.

The chatbot cannot access a customer's private live order status. For exact tracking, customers should check Profile > My Orders or contact support with the order number.
