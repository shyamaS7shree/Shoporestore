# Purchase Guide

To purchase a product on Shopore:

1. Search for a brand or product from the navbar, or browse categories.
2. Open the product page.
3. Select size, color, or quantity if the product asks for it.
4. Click Add to Bag.
5. Open Bag from the navbar.
6. Review product details, quantity, and price.
7. Continue to checkout.
8. Add or confirm delivery details.
9. Complete payment.

Customers can manage their account, orders, and wishlist from Profile after login.
